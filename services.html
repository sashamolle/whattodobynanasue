1:1 Milestone Consultations

For parents with concerns about their baby's mobility.

If you have that "gut feeling" something is off, or you're worried about a specific milestone, a 1:1 consultation provides a full assessment and a personalized plan to get your baby back on track.

When to seek help?

  • Baby consistently dislikes tummy time or avoids it.
  • Not rolling by 6-7 months of age.
  • Seems "stuck" or frustrated with movement.
  • Skipping crawling or crawling in an unusual pattern.
  • Any other specific mobility concerns you may have.

What to expect:

  • A comprehensive 60-minute assessment of your baby's movement patterns.
  • A detailed discussion of your specific goals and concerns.
  • Demonstration of gentle, effective techniques and exercises.
  • A personalized take-home plan with clear steps.
