My most important title is "Nana"

When my grandson was born abroad, I found myself trying to guide my daughter through his mobility milestones from thousands of miles away. Even with decades of experience, I realized I needed a clear blueprint to help them succeed from a distance. I distilled my 35 years of knowledge into a simple, actionable system.

Why This Guidance Matters

As Swedens's leading expert in pediatric musculoskeletal health, I’ve spent 35 years filling the gap in standard primary care for infants and children. My philosophy combines high-level professional knowledge with a practical, reassuring approach:

  • Structured Guidance: Clear and gentle, step-by-step guidnace for tummy time, rolling, sitting and crawling.
  • Proactive Play: Simple, proactive play techniques that support your baby's strong, symmetrical motor development.
  • Enduring Empowerment: Learn how to work with your baby to help them achieve their milestones.

This blueprint is the culmination of three decades of work and a grandmother's love—the definitive guide to unlocking your baby’s full physical potential.
