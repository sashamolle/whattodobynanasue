My most important title is "Nana"

When my grandson was born abroad, I found myself trying to guide my daughter through his mobility milestones from thousands of miles away. Even with decades of experience, I realized I needed a clear blueprint to help them succeed from a distance. I distilled my 35 years of knowledge into a simple, actionable system—this book.

Why This Guidance Matters

As Swedens's leading expert in pediatric musculoskeletal health, I’ve spent 35 years filling the gap in standard primary care for infants and children. My philosophy combines high-level professional knowledge with a practical, reassuring approach:

  • Proactive Play for Mobility: Simple strategies that support optimal motor development.
  • A Whole Lot of Encouragement: Easing worries with evidence-based guidance.

This book is the culmination of three decades of work and a grandmother's love—the definitive guide to unlocking your baby’s full physical potential.

Why Early Mobility Matters

Every parent has the power to support their child's natural development. Focusing on early physical development is the foundation for all future learning. By systematically engaging and strengthening key motor patterns from an early age, you help your baby develop a strong, symmetrical core for future spinal health. This symmetrical movement stimulates both sides of the brain equally, setting your baby up for a lifetime of enjoying healthy movement.
