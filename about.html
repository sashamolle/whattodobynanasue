Meet Nana Sue

Dr. Sue A. Weber, BSc, DC MSc. Specialist in Pediatric Musculoskeletal Health

I believe every parent has the power to support their child's natural development—they just need to know *what to do*. And that’s exactly where my journey to write this book began.

My most important title is "Nana."

When my grandson was born in another country, I suddenly found myself trying to guide my daughter through the crucial stages of his mobility milestone development from thousands of miles away. It was then I realized that even with decades of professional experience, I needed a clear, practical guide—a blueprint—to help her (and him) succeed from a distance. I had to distill my 35 years of knowledge into a simple, actionable system.

That system is now this book.

Why This Guidance Matters

As Swedens's leading expert in pediatric musculoskeletal health and Sweden’s most experienced practitioner in this area, I’ve spent the last 35 years focused exclusively on pregnant women, infants, children, and adolescents. My work has shown me that the musculoskeletal care these groups need—the foundation for strong motor development—is often missed in standard primary care. My practice, research, and education are dedicated to filling that critical gap.

My philosophy combines high-level professional knowledge with the practical, reassuring approach you deserve:

  • Structured Guidance: Clear and gentle, step-by-step guidnace for tummy time, rolling, sitting and crawling.
  • Proactive Play: Simple, proactive play techniques that support your baby's strong, symmetrical motor development.
  • Enduring Empowerment: Learn how to work with your baby to help them achieve their milestones.

My research has centered on complex issues like the relationship between cervical spine dysfunction and headaches in children. Now, I’m focused on translating that high-level expertise into accessible, actionable guidance for you. This book is the culmination of three decades of work and a grandmother's love—it’s the definitive guide to unlocking your baby’s full physical potential.

I’m here to answer your questions, ease your worries, and give you the confidence and tools to help your baby thrive.
